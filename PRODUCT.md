# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal staff of A&A Inmobiliaria (admins/agents), not the public. They manage the property catalog, leads, sales, financing terms, and their small team from a laptop/desktop during work hours. The job to be done: publish and edit listings fast, triage WhatsApp leads, track sales and financing plans, without friction or visual noise getting in the way.

## Product Purpose

Internal CRM/catalog manager for a Honduran real estate brokerage. It is the backstage tool that feeds the public marketplace (aabienes.com). Success looks like: an agent can list a property, edit it, or check today's leads in under a minute, and the tool itself signals that A&A is a serious, well-run operation (not a scrappy side project) every time staff open it.

## Brand Personality

Precise, minimal, quietly expensive. Reference: Linear / Stripe Dashboard — typography and spacing carry the hierarchy, color is almost absent except for one deliberate accent per screen. Cold and exact rather than warm and decorative.

## Anti-references

- Generic free-SaaS admin templates (default Bootstrap/Ant Design, colorful icon soup, identical repeated cards).
- Gold overused as decoration — the brand accent (#D4B254) must be reserved for the single most important action or state per screen, not sprayed across every button, badge, and border.
- Dense spreadsheet-style tables with no visual hierarchy.

## Design Principles

- One accent, used once: gold marks the primary action or the active state, nothing else competes with it.
- Hierarchy through type and space, not color or decoration.
- Every screen has exactly one obvious next action.
- Dark and light are both first-class; neither is the "real" theme.
- Restraint reads as expensive; ornamentation reads as cheap.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA (4.5:1 text contrast) in both themes. Existing light/dark toggle must remain functional and equally polished in both directions. No motion-only feedback (respect prefers-reduced-motion where animation is added).
